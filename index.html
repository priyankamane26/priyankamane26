Digital Watermarking for Video Piracy Detection

It aimed at coping the problem of digital piracy by using the ‘Secure Spread Spectrum Watermarking based on Chaotic Map for still’ algorithm. This project was built in technologies like JAVA, HTML, CSS and has two fragments, first, core activity of detecting piracy in video, allowing original images to be digitally watermarked and extended functionality of detecting piracy in images and audio was part of a desktop application ‘Piracy Shield’. Second, This desktop application was made available to only Valid users to further qualify the possibility of unauthorised user. With additional feature to maintain the profile, contact details and feedback portal to ensure quality of the product is at high importance.
